Frequently asked

If yours is not answered here, call or email and we will happily talk it through.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you don't need to see a doctor first. If you have already seen one, please bring along any scans, reports or letters. A referral is only required for WorkCover, Veterans' Affairs, CTP (third party insurance) or a Medicare Chronic Disease Management plan.

What do I need to bring to my first consultation?

Any scan or blood test reports, a written list of medications if you take several, and your doctor's referral if you have one.

Why should I choose Holistic Physio?

We offer truly personalised care that combines physiotherapy, acupuncture, Chinese herbal medicine and clinical Pilates. By listening to your story and tailoring treatment to your needs, we focus on treating the root cause of your discomfort, not just the symptoms.

Can I claim my treatments on private health fund?

Yes. We are registered with all the health funds for physiotherapy, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine, so you can claim for each modality if it's covered by your policy.

What services do you offer?

Beyond traditional physiotherapy, we provide acupuncture, Chinese herbal medicine and clinical Pilates, supporting acute and chronic pain, migraines, sports injuries, women's health and stress-related issues.

Do I need a referral for physiotherapy appointments?

In most cases, no. The exception is if you're claiming through Medicare's Chronic Disease Management plan, WorkCover, the Department of Veterans' Affairs, or certain third-party insurance providers.

What are the payment facilities available?

EFTPOS is available at the clinic, so you can pay by bank card, credit card or cash. Health fund claims go on the spot through our HICAPS terminal, so you only pay the gap.

Can I claim from Medicare for any of my treatments?

Medicare's Chronic Disease Management plan covers 5 physiotherapy consultations per calendar year with a GP referral. Consultations are not bulk-billed, you pay in full and the Medicare portion of $61.80 is refunded directly to your bank account via HICAPS.

Where is Holistic Physio located?

26 Gladstone Rd (corner of Brook St), Highgate Hill Qld 4101. If you have time before or after your appointment, take a walk to Highgate Hill Park, stroll along the Brisbane River, or relax at one of the nearby cafes.
