What is physiotherapy?

A science-based practice that helps people restore movement, strength and function after injury or illness. Using manual therapy, tailored exercise programs, posture correction and education, physiotherapy supports recovery and helps prevent future problems.

Good questions

Nervous about your first physio session? Here's what settles most people.

Will physiotherapy hurt?

Treatment is adjusted to your comfort throughout. You are always in control, and we check in as we go rather than pushing through pain.

What if I don't know what's actually wrong?

That's normal, most people don't. The first session is a thorough assessment to work out what's going on before any treatment plan is set.

How many sessions will I need?

It depends on your condition, but many people notice a helpful shift within the first few visits. We'll give you a realistic estimate after your assessment.

What should I wear?

Comfortable clothing you can move in. We'll let you know if anything specific helps for your particular area of concern.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you can book directly. A referral is only required for WorkCover, Veterans' Affairs, CTP or a Medicare Chronic Disease Management plan.

Can I claim on my private health fund?

In most cases, yes. The clinic is registered with all major health funds for physiotherapy, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine, so most policies allow you to claim on the spot through HICAPS and just pay the gap. Check with your fund to confirm your level of cover.
