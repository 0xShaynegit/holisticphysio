Good questions

Fertility struggles can feel overwhelming. Here's what tends to reassure people.

How long before trying to conceive should we start?

Many people start around 3 months before trying or beginning IVF, but we adapt to whatever timeline you're on, including if you've already started.

Will this interfere with my IVF specialist's plan?

No, we work collaboratively alongside your fertility specialist, timing treatment to support your existing plan, not replace it.

Is it normal to feel emotional talking about this?

Completely. This is a private, unhurried space, and Sandra has supported hundreds of couples through this journey, including her own.

Can my partner be involved too?

Yes, around half of fertility challenges involve male factors, and we welcome partners to be assessed and supported too.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you can book directly. A referral is only required for WorkCover, Veterans' Affairs, CTP or a Medicare Chronic Disease Management plan.

Can I claim on my private health fund?

In most cases, yes. The clinic is registered with all major health funds for physiotherapy, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine, so most policies allow you to claim on the spot through HICAPS and just pay the gap. Check with your fund to confirm your level of cover.
