Good questions

Trying herbal medicine for the first time raises real questions. Here are the common ones.

Is it safe to take with my other medications?

We take a full medical history before prescribing, including any medications you're on, and formulas are chosen to work safely alongside them.

Will the herbs be unpleasant to take?

Formulas are provided as capsules, so there's no bitter tea to choke down, just part of your normal routine.

How long until I notice a difference?

Herbal medicine works gradually. Many people notice initial changes within a few weeks, with fuller benefits building over a couple of months.

Is it safe during pregnancy?

Some formulas are appropriate during pregnancy and some are not. This is always discussed individually based on your stage and health history.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you can book directly. A referral is only required for WorkCover, Veterans' Affairs, CTP or a Medicare Chronic Disease Management plan.

Can I claim on my private health fund?

In most cases, yes. The clinic is registered with all major health funds for physiotherapy, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine, so most policies allow you to claim on the spot through HICAPS and just pay the gap. Check with your fund to confirm your level of cover.
