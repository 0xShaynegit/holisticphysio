Good questions

If scans have come back clear but the headaches haven't stopped, you're not alone. Here's what to know.

My scans came back clear, can you still help?

Yes. Many headaches trace back to neck tension, posture or hormonal patterns that don't show up on a scan but respond well to treatment.

Will acupuncture trigger a migraine?

It's rare. Most people find acupuncture calming rather than triggering, and treatment is adjusted if you're sensitive to stimulation.

How many sessions before headaches reduce?

Many people notice fewer or milder headaches within 4 to 6 sessions, though this varies with how long the pattern has been established.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you can book directly. A referral is only required for WorkCover, Veterans' Affairs, CTP or a Medicare Chronic Disease Management plan.

Can I claim on my private health fund?

In most cases, yes. The clinic is registered with all major health funds for physiotherapy, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine, so most policies allow you to claim on the spot through HICAPS and just pay the gap. Check with your fund to confirm your level of cover.
