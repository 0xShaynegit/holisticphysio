Is it awkward to discuss women's health concerns?

No, these conversations happen every day here and are treated with the same care as any other health concern.

Can I come in while on my period or pregnant?

Yes, treatment is simply adapted to your cycle or stage of pregnancy, there's no need to reschedule around it.

Can this help with menopause naturally?

Yes, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine are commonly used to ease hot flushes, mood changes and sleep disruption through menopause.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

No, you can book directly. A referral is only required for WorkCover, Veterans' Affairs, CTP or a Medicare Chronic Disease Management plan.

Can I claim on my private health fund?

In most cases, yes. The clinic is registered with all major health funds for physiotherapy, acupuncture and Chinese herbal medicine, so most policies allow you to claim on the spot through HICAPS and just pay the gap. Check with your fund to confirm your level of cover.
